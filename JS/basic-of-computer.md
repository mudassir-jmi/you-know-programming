ğŸš€ Computers Are Electronic Machines:- The computer uses electricity, not mechanical parts, for its data processing and storage.

ğŸš€ One probable reason is that we have ten fingers to represent numbers. The computer has switches to represent data and switches have only two states: ON and OFF.

ğŸš€ The evolution of how switches were built made computers faster, cheaper, and smaller.
